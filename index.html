Help & Support

How to Add a Tenant?

Go to the Tenants section, fill in the tenant's name, room/unit number, rent amount, and due date. Click "Add Tenant" to save.

How to Track Payments?

Navigate to the Payments section to see all tenants and their payment status. Click "Mark Paid" when a tenant has paid their rent.

How to Manage Notifications?

Go to the Notifications section to view all notifications and manage your notification preferences.

Contact Support

If you need additional help, please contact our support team at support@rentify.com or call 1-800-RENTIFY.

FAQ

Q: Can I edit tenant information?
A: Currently, you can delete and re-add tenants. Edit functionality will be available in a future update.

Q: How do I change my password?
A: Please use the Firebase authentication system to reset your password through the login page.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes, all data is stored securely in Firebase and is only accessible by you.
